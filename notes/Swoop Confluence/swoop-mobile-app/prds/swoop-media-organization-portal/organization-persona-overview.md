## Partner Persona Overview

### Who They Are

The typical SWOOP Media partner is an **event organizer** responsible for running sports tournaments or competitions at the local, regional, state, or national level. Their operations range from small weekend events to large multi-day championships. Some are physically on-site during events; others manage events remotely through staff or venue partners.

### Core Responsibilities

- Scheduling and managing tournaments
- Communicating with teams, parents, and coaches
- Coordinating vendors (venues, referees, trainers, media, etc.)
- Overseeing financial performance for each event
- Delivering promised experiences and assets to participants
### Why They Use SWOOP Media

These partners have been sold on SWOOP Media as a way to:

- **Generate new revenue** through photo sales at their events
- **Improve the event experience** with high-quality photography
- **Deliver marketing assets** for post-event promotion, sponsors, and social media
- **Reduce operational overhead** by letting SWOOP handle media capture and distribution
### What They Expect From the Portal

- **Financial Visibility**: They want to know exactly how much revenue their events are generating and what their payout share is on an event-by-event basis.
- **Media Access**: They need the ability to download the marketing assets SWOOP captures&mdash;photos for social media, highlight reels, sponsor recaps, and more.
- **Event Management**: They want to submit new events or import existing ones, see which events are scheduled, in progress, or completed, and confirm that SWOOP is covering them.
- **Operational Confidence**: They expect a clear, centralized interface that shows event status, upload progress, and the overall performance of their relationship with SWOOP.
### Their Relationship to the Fastbreak Ecosystem

Partners fall into two categories:

- **Existing Fastbreak Customers**

- 
Already use Fastbreak products like ticketing, scheduling, travel, or Compete
- Already have events and organizational data in the ecosystem
- Expect seamless login and shared data across products
- **New-to-Fastbreak Customers**

- 
Enter the ecosystem specifically through SWOOP Media
- Need an onboarding flow to create their organization profile
- Will learn the Fastbreak platform through their SWOOP experience
The portal must support both personas equally&mdash;whether they are returning customers syncing existing events or first-time partners creating everything from scratch.
